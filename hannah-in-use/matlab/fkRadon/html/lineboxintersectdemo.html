lineboxintersectdemo

synopsis

demonstrates lineboxintersect function

syntax

    LINEBOXINTERSECTDEMO
    LINEBOXINTERSECTDEMO(box)
    LINEBOXINTERSECTDEMO(box,theta)
    LINEBOXINTERSECTDEMO(box,theta,rho)

description

LINEBOXINTERSECTDEMO plots a 1x1 box in the upper plot and theta-rho space in the lower plot. By clicking in the lower plot, the line defined by the theta, rho pair at that point is drawn in the upper plot.

LINEBOXINTERSECTDEMO(box) specify your own box in [xleft xright ybottom ytop] format.

LINEBOXINTERSECTDEMO(box,theta) specify the theta range (default=[-0.5 0.5]*pi)

LINEBOXINTERSECTDEMO(box,theta,rho) specify the rho range.
